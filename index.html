Habit Swap — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

Habit Swap collects the following information when you create an account:

  • Email address (for registered accounts)
  • Swap activity data (urge types, completion status, timestamps)
  • App usage statistics (streak data, points)

Guest users are assigned an anonymous identifier. No personal information is collected in guest mode.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Show you your own progress and history
  • Calculate your Brain Rewire percentage and streak
  • Schedule local notifications at your danger window time

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond providing the app's core functionality.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Supabase's privacy policy is available at supabase.com/privacy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties. We do not use any advertising networks or analytics platforms that collect personal data.

5. Notifications

Habit Swap uses local notifications only. We do not use push notification servers. All notifications are scheduled on your device and do not transmit any data.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen within the app. Deletion is permanent and immediate.

7. Children's Privacy

Habit Swap is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR (European users) or other applicable privacy laws to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
